My name is Clare Bruning, and I am pursuing a Web Design and Development degree at FVTC. I graduated from Lawrence University with a B.A. in English and Creative Writing in 2016, but my interest in web content and desire for a more specialized set of technical skills led me to return to school for a second undergrad. I work full time as a shift manager at ALDI, so balancing school with work takes up the majority of my time. When not engaged in either of those activities, I love to create meal plans, grocery shop, cook, eat and feed my family. I also love to read and watch TV shows and movies on occasion. I ran cross country and track competitively for 11 years, all through college, and I really needed a break after graduating. Enough time has passed now that I enjoy running again, more for fitness and the fun of it than training for races anymore.
